Hi, I'm Chiza!

Chemist turned teacher with a passion for blending science, business, and faith!

Vision

I want to live a life that reflects a greater purpose—one where love, growth, and harmony guide me.

I believe we’re all part of God’s story, working to create a world where love, justice, and grace can flourish, while staying true to the path He’s set for us.

For me, science, spirituality, and faith aren’t separate—they come together to show how everything in life is deeply connected and meant to help us grow and transform.

I hope to live a balanced life, where I take responsibility for my choices, work with others, and build stronger connections with God, my community, and the world around me.

Even when life feels uncertain, I hold on to hope—trusting in God’s promise of redemption and striving to bring harmony between the physical and spiritual parts of life.

Values

  • God at the Center: I see God in everything and try to focus on growing spiritually and reflecting on His presence in my life.
  • Choose Love: I believe love is the most powerful force, helping us grow individually and together, while pushing past fear and division.
  • Freedom with Responsibility: God gives us free will, and I try to use it wisely, making choices that matter for me, others, and His bigger plan.
  • Grace in Growth: I accept that challenges and mistakes are part of life—they’re opportunities to learn, grow, and trust in God’s plan for me.
  • Life as Sacred: Life is a place to grow and learn, but it’s also a chance to live out God’s love and purpose in everyday moments.
  • Faith Meets Curiosity: I want to bridge what I learn from the Bible with what I discover through science and spirituality, finding wisdom in both.
  • Seek Purpose: Through prayer, mindfulness, and reflection, I try to better understand God’s will and my role in His story.
  • Hopeful Living: I trust in God’s promises, even when the future feels uncertain, and I try to make choices that reflect faith and love.
  • Together in Grace: Building relationships and communities that reflect God’s love is important to me—I value unity, compassion, and teamwork.
  • Respecting Others: Everyone’s experience and perspective is unique, and I try to honor that while staying rooted in God’s truth.
  • Embrace the Unknown: I’m open to the mystery of God’s creation, knowing there’s more to life than what we see or understand, and trusting in His purpose for it all.
